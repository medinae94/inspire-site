The App Development Process

Analyze

First things first. We'll study the market and check whether there's any demand for your idea. If there is a demand we'll carefully research your competitors, analyzing their strengths and weakness.


This stage in the process will also consist of a technical feasibility assessment in which we will work together to determine all project features as well as set up project milestones.

1

Prototyping & Design

We divide this stage of the process into the following steps:

Creating a sketch - Draft version of your app on paper that sets up number of screens and how they'll interact with each other

Creating wireframes - Provides the visualization of the draft app structure

Creating a clickable prototype - Helps to find & analyze all possible use cases. Generally, after this stage, we may discover logical breaks and technical inconsistencies in your idea. Don't be discouraged. The more issues found prior to coding, the more time and money you save.

2

Development

Awesome! We've reached the most exciting and challenging stage of the process. This is where we decided whether to stick to the native app(iOS/Android)or to develop a cross-platform one. At Insp;re, we develop native apps, which means that in the end you have your custom app for every operating system.

3

Testing, Publishing, and Maintenance

This stage begins with testing whether all code parts of your app work correctly together. All security issues are carefully checked to protect the privacy of your users.


Once your app passes testing, it's time to publish on the app store and show it to the world. You can do this yourself, or ask us to assist you.


Even now it's too early to relax. After publishing, there may still remain bugs to be eliminated. We ensure all projects with three months of free bug fixing.
