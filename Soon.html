What is Insp;re Tech?

Insp;re Tech began as nothing but an idea in the imagination of our CEO, like a seed planted in the dirt you could say. How could one blend together the passion for helping and inspiring others with the passion of a developer? The answer: create a brand where a percentage of each client's overall fee is donated, in their name, to Project Semicolon or a charity of their choice.
